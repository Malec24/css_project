Garner State Park

This camp site was located at Garner State Park, a west Texas state park.
Garner state's water is crystal clear...you can see straight to the bottom with the only obstruction coming from the water's ripples.
I highly recommend visiting.

Things to do there:

  1. Kayaking
  2. Hiking
  3. Grilling
  4. fishing
  5. Bird watching


Garnerstatepark.com

Birds you may see at Garner State Park:

Golden-cheeked warblers
Currently on the endangered species list
Black-capped vireo
Recently taken off the endangered species list

Enchanted Rock


This trip was to Enchanted Rock. Enchanted rock is a great mild hiking spot, perfect for beginners.
Along the hike you'll encounter wildlife and enjoy the beautiful views of the west Texas hill country...
..all that fun can make it easy to forget to hydrate, be sure to bring plenty of water!

West Texas Road Trip

This photo was taken on a west Texas road trip.
The road trip itself was a spontaneous, totally unplanned adventure.
When we stopped for rest, we simply pulled over and camped out on the side of the road.
It is incredibly quiet out there and no civilization for miles.


What you will need on a camping trip in west Texas:

  • Tent
  • Sleeping bag
  • Sunscreen
  • Water
  • Hiking shoes
  • Clothing appropriate for high temperatures
  • Fruit and non perishable foods
  • Toilet paper
  • Multipurpose tool
  • Bug repellent
  • Hat or sunglasses
